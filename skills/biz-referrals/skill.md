# biz-referrals

**Purpose:** Turn every client touchpoint into a referral engine. Referral leads convert 5-8x better than cold outreach.

---

## I0 — Four stages to ask

| Stage | When | Script |
|-------|------|--------|
| **Warm network** | Before you start actively selling | *"I'm starting to take on [niche] clients. If you know anyone who [specific problem], I'd love an intro."* |
| **After discovery call** | Prospect says "not now" or "not a fit" | *"Totally fair. If you know someone who'd be a better fit for this, I'd appreciate the introduction."* |
| **At kickoff** | Client just signed | *"One quick ask: if someone comes to mind who needs this same help, I'd love to chat with them too."* |
| **After delivery** | Client is happy with results | *"This worked because of [specific outcome]. Who else do you know who'd benefit from the same?"* |

---

## I1 — Make it easy to refer you

- **Write a one-paragraph blurb** your clients can forward as-is.
- **Create a "who this is for" doc** (1 page) they can share.
- **Offer a referral bonus** (10-15% of first month / flat finder's fee).
- **Send a calendar link** so referred leads book directly — no back-and-forth.

**Referral assets to prepare:**
- [ ] 2-sentence pitch they can paste in a DM
- [ ] 1-page PDF: "Who I help, what I solve, how it works"
- [ ] Referral tracking link (e.g. `you.com?ref=CLIENTNAME`)

---

## I2 — Tracking referrals

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| Source contact | Who referred |
| Referred contact | Who was referred |
| Stage | Introduced / Meeting booked / Proposal / Closed |
| Value | Deal size if closed |

**Review weekly:** How many referrals asked for? How many given? Conversion rate?

---

## I3 — Why referrals win

| Channel | Typical close rate |
|---------|-------------------|
| Cold outbound | 1-3% |
| Inbound (content) | 5-10% |
| **Referral** | **25-40%** |

Referrals skip the trust-building phase entirely. The referrer's credibility transfers to you.
