# Pipeline Tracker

## Funnel Stages

1. **Awareness** — They know you exist
2. **Interest** — They engage with your content or profile
3. **Conversation** — DM, email, or call
4. **Discovery** — You ran a discovery call
5. **Proposal** — Proposal sent
6. **Negotiation** — Discussing scope/price
7. **Closed Won** — Signed + deposit
8. **Closed Lost** — Not a fit
