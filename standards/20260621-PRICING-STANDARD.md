# Pricing Standard

Binding rules for pricing engagements. All `biz-pricing`, `biz-proposal`, and `biz-discovery` output must comply.

## Pricing model

1. **Fixed price only.** Never hourly billing. Hourly rates punish efficiency and create misaligned incentives.
2. **Price is anchored to value, not cost.** The price reflects the business outcome for the client, not the time spent.
3. **One primary offer with one price.** Optional tiers (e.g., "Core / Complete / Premium") are allowed, but there must be a recommended option.

## Payment terms

1. **50% upfront, 50% on completion** unless local regulation or client risk profile requires otherwise.
2. **Milestone-gated payments** for engagements longer than 30 days:
   - 25% kickoff
   - 25% midpoint milestone
   - 50% final delivery
3. **Net-15 invoicing** for the final payment; net-30 only when client policy requires it.
4. **Currency:** USD for international clients; local currency for domestic clients.

## Scope rules

1. **In-scope items** are listed explicitly in the proposal.
2. **Out-of-scope items** are listed explicitly to prevent scope creep.
3. **Assumptions** are documented; if an assumption fails, it triggers a change order.
4. **Change order process:** any scope change requires written client approval and a price/time adjustment before work proceeds.

## Risk management

1. **Liability cap** = total contract value, unless a higher amount is standard for the industry and insured.
2. **No unlimited liability.** Reject or renegotiate clauses that expose the operator to uncapped damages.
3. **Payment before delivery of final assets.** Hold back editable source files or final report until final payment clears.

## Proposal pricing format

Every proposal must include:

| Item | Detail |
|------|--------|
| **Offer** | One-line description of the engagement |
| **Price** | Fixed price, currency, payment schedule |
| **Scope** | Bullet list of in-scope deliverables |
| **Out of scope** | Bullet list of excluded work |
| **Timeline** | Start date, milestones, completion date |
| **Assumptions** | Client responsibilities and prerequisites |
| **Next step** | What the client must do to approve and begin |

## Anti-patterns

- Quoting hourly rates.
- Sending a proposal without a price.
- Scope creep without a change order.
- Starting work before the deposit clears.
- Liability cap higher than contract value without insurance.
