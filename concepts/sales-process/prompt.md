# BIZ-05: Sales Process

## Questions to explore

1. What is your current sales process? (Write it end-to-end)
2. At which stage do deals most often stall? (Diagnose bottleneck)
3. What information do you lack when entering each stage?
4. What could you do to move stalled deals forward?
5. What would an ideal sales process look like?

## Output

- Current sales process map
- Bottleneck analysis
- Improved sales process
- Scripts / templates per stage
