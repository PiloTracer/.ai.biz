# BIZ-04: Brand Positioning

## Questions to explore

1. What is the ONE thing you want to be known for? (The unified offer)
2. Who is the ONE person you want to reach? (Target buyer)
3. What makes you different? (The unfair advantage)
4. What is the story that connects you to your buyer? (Narrative)
5. What does your current brand say vs. what you want it to say? (Audit)

## Output

- Positioning statement (1 sentence)
- Key messaging pillars (3-4)
- Brand voice guidelines
- Before/after brand audit
