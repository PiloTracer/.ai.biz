# BIZ-03: Channel Strategy

## Questions to explore

1. Where does your target buyer spend time? (LinkedIn, communities, events, podcasts)
2. What content do they consume? (Technical, strategic, industry-specific)
3. What trust-building channels work for your offer? (Content, referrals, talks)
4. What channels are you already strong in? (Network, existing platform)
5. Which channel gives the best ROI for your time? (Cost per conversation)

## Output

- Primary channel recommendation
- Secondary channel list
- Weekly time allocation per channel
- 30-day channel experiment plan
