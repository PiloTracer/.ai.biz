# BIZ-01: Market Fit

## Questions to explore

1. Who exactly has this problem? (Be specific — industry, role, company size)
2. How are they solving it today? (Workaround, competitor, doing nothing)
3. What's the pain of NOT solving it? (Financial, operational, emotional)
4. Are they actively looking for a solution? (Searching, asking, buying)
5. Will they pay to solve it? (Have budget, have authority)

## Output

- Target buyer profile (1 paragraph)
- Problem validation (confirmed/inferred/unknown)
- Willingness to pay assessment
- Go / No-go recommendation
