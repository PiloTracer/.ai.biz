# BIZ-02: Pricing Model

## Questions to explore

1. What is the value to the client? (Revenue enabled, cost saved, risk avoided)
2. What are the alternatives and their costs? (Agency, freelancer, in-house, doing nothing)
3. What is your effort-based floor? (Time × rate + overhead + buffer)
4. What does the market bear? (Competitor pricing, industry norms)
5. What pricing model fits? (Fixed price, milestone, retainer, value-based)

## Output

- Price range recommendation
- Pricing model recommendation
- Payment terms proposal
- Upsell / expansion opportunities
